Practical Advice

Essential Travel Tips

🎫
UNESCO Ticket
Buy the Cultural Triangle ticket at your first site — it covers both Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa and saves significant money.
👘
Dress Code
Shoulders and knees must be covered at all temples. Carry a sarong as backup. Remove shoes at most sacred sites.
🛺
Transport Tips
Anuradhapura: negotiate a full-day tuk-tuk rate (2,500–3,500 LKR). Polonnaruwa: rent a bicycle (300–500 LKR/day) — far better for the flat terrain.
☀️
Beat the Heat
Start mornings early (7–8 AM) and plan for the 12–2 PM heat. Carry water at all times and wear light, breathable clothing.
📸
Photography
No selfies with Buddha statues — it is strictly enforced. No flash photography inside Thivanka Image House. Evening Ruwanweli is spectacular for photos.
💰
Budget Guide
Tuk-tuk full day: 2,500–3,500 LKR. Bicycle: 300–500 LKR. Local lunch: 500–1,200 LKR. Entry tickets: covered by UNESCO pass.
