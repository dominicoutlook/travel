10:00 AM
Anuradhapura Railway Station ArrivalMap
Store luggage at the station cloakroom. Hire a tuk-tuk outside and negotiate a full-day rate (≈ 2,500–3,500 LKR). Head straight to the ancient city.

Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi ⭐Map
World's oldest documented sacred tree (288 BC). Remove shoes at the counter outside. Calm and spiritually powerful mid-morning. 45 min.
11:10 AM
Lovamahapaya (Brazen Palace)Map
2-min walk from Bodhi Tree. 1,600 ancient stone pillars of a once 9-storey palace. Quick but impressive. 20 min.
11:35 AM
Ruwanweli Maha Seya ⭐Map
Sri Lanka's most iconic white stupa. Walk the full perimeter — don't miss the elephant wall at the base. Brilliant in midday light. 55 min.
12:45 PM
Lunch Local Restaurant
🍛 Sorriso Restaurant ★ 4.8  ·  Sri Lankan Buffet  ·  Open 7:30 AM–10 PM
Delicious rice & curry buffet with fresh juices. Unique outdoor setting built around a tree. 45 min.
1:45 PM
ThuparamayaMap
Sri Lanka's oldest stupa. Quieter and more peaceful than the main sites. Unique concentric stone pillars. 30 min.
2:15 PM
Miridiya Lake Resort — Check-in 🏨Map
Drop bags, freshen up. Collect tuk-tuk for the afternoon session.
3:30 PM
JetavanaramayaMap
One of the world's tallest ancient brick structures. Glows beautifully in afternoon light. 50 min.
4:30 PM
Mirisawetiya StupaMap
Peaceful lakeside stupa near Tissa Wewa. Built by King Dutugemunu. Close to hotel — easy return for dinner. 45 min.
💡 Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi, Lovamahapaya & Ruwanweli are all within 500m of each other — walk between them to save time.
Day 02
North Ruins & Evening Magic
🏨 Miridiya Lake Resort · Breakfast & Dinner
Full Day Route 🚗 Driving
🏛️ Abhayagiri→ 🧘 Samadhi Statue→ 🌿 Ranmasu Uyana→ 🍛 Little Paradise (Lunch)→ 🧱 Jetavanaramaya→ 🌇 Ruwanweli (Evening)
Open in Google Maps
8:00 AM
Abhayagiriya DagobaMap
Arrive early while it's cool. Sri Lanka's 2nd largest stupa. The on-site museum has incredible excavated artefacts. 60 min.
9:15 AM
Samadhi Buddha Statue ⭐Map
The most serene Buddha in Sri Lanka — carved from a single block of dolomite marble. Morning light is perfect here. 600m south of Abhayagiri. 45 min.
10:15 AM
Ranmasu Uyana (Royal Park)Map
Ancient 40-acre royal pleasure garden with advanced water engineering. Don't miss the mysterious Sakwala Chakraya star gate carving at the back. 60 min.
12:00 PM
Lunch Local Restaurant
🍛 Little Paradise Restaurant ★ 4.8  ·  Traditional Rice & Curry  ·  Open 8 AM–9:30 PM
Best traditional Sri Lankan meal in Anuradhapura. Set menu in a peaceful garden guesthouse. 45 min.
1:15 PM
Jetavanaramaya (Museum)Map
Explore the on-site museum with incredible excavated artefacts from this once 120m-tall stupa. 60 min.
5:00 PM
Ruwanweli Maha Seya — Evening Visit ⭐⭐Map
Return for the evening experience. The stupa is softly lit at dusk with chanting and incense. One of the most magical sights in Sri Lanka — absolutely do not miss this. 60 min.
🌅 The evening Ruwanweli visit at 5 PM is one of the most unforgettable experiences of the entire trip. Plan your day around it.

Royal Palace & Sacred Quadrangle
🏨 Hotel Sudu Araliya · 🚲 Hire bicycle (300–500 LKR/day)
Full Day Cycling Route 🚲 Bicycle
🏯 Royal Palace→ 🛁 Kumara Pokuna→ 🪷 Nissanka→ ✨ Vatadage→ 🕌 Hatadage→ 🍛 HWA HWA (Lunch)→ 🗿 Menik Vehera→ 💎 Pabalu Vehera→ 🌍 Ancient City
Open in Google Maps
8:00 AM
Royal Palace of King Parakramabahu ⭐Map
Once 7 storeys tall with 1,000 chambers. Arrive at opening time (8 AM) for fewer crowds. The scale of the remaining brick walls is extraordinary. 50 min.
9:00 AM
Kumara Pokuna (Royal Bath)Map
3-min walk south. Royal bathing pond with carved lion heads. Built below palace level so water flows naturally by gravity. 30 min.
9:40 AM
Nissanka Latha MandapayaMap
The king's pirith-chanting pavilion — famous for its unique lotus-stalk curved granite pillars. Unlike anything else in Sri Lanka. 30 min.
10:20 AM
Vatadage ⭐⭐Map
The most exquisite circular relic house in Sri Lanka. Study each of the 4 carved entrances carefully. The stonework is remarkable. Allow extra time here. 50 min.
11:20 AM
Hetadage (Hatadage)
Right beside Vatadage in the Sacred Quadrangle. Tooth Relic shrine with standing Buddhas still in place. 30 min.
12:00 PM
Lunch Local Restaurant
🍛 HWA HWA Eating House ★ 4.8  ·  5-min bicycle ride from Sacred Quadrangle
45 min.
1:00 PM
Menik Vehera
Oldest stupa in Polonnaruwa with unique lion relief carvings. Often quiet and uncrowded. 40 min.
1:50 PM
Pabalu Vehera
Named after glass beads found during excavations. Built by Queen Rupavati, consort of King Parakramabahu. 40 min.
2:40 PM
Polonnaruwa Ancient City — UNESCO Zone
Cycle through the main archaeological zone. Well-maintained paths between temple clusters. Keep your entry ticket — it's checked at multiple points. 60 min.
🚲 A bicycle is the best way to explore Polonnaruwa. Flat terrain, great paths, and you can stop anywhere. Rent from outside the hotel for 300–500 LKR/day.
Day 05
Northern Temples & Farewell Sunset
🏨 Hotel Sudu Araliya · 🚲 Continue on bicycle
Full Day Cycling Route 🚲 Bicycle
🏛️ Lankathilaka→ ✨ Gal Viharaya→ 🎨 Thivanka→ 🍛 Silver Line (Lunch)→ 🏨 Hotel→ 🌅 Parakrama Samudra
Open in Google Maps
8:00 AM
Lankatilaka Temple
A towering image house with a 41-foot headless standing Buddha framed by massive brick walls. One of the most striking sights in Polonnaruwa. 60 min.
9:15 AM
Gal Viharaya ⭐⭐⭐
The absolute crown jewel of Polonnaruwa. Four colossal Buddhas carved from a single granite face. Arrive before 10 AM to beat tour groups. Note: No selfies with the Buddha — strictly enforced by guards. 90 min.
11:00 AM
Thivanka Image House
Finest surviving Polonnaruwa-era frescoes inside. The triple-bent Buddha pose ('Thivanka') is unique in Sri Lankan art. No flash photography. 60 min.
12:15 PM
Lunch Local Restaurant
🍛 Silver Line Restaurant ★ 4.8  ·  Fresh Clay-Pot Dishes  ·  Open 11 AM–6 PM
Market-to-table experience with beautiful surroundings. Highly recommended for vegetarians too. 45 min.
1:30 PM
Hotel Sudu Araliya — Rest & Pool 🏊
Well-deserved afternoon rest. Enjoy the hotel pool overlooking the great lake.
4:30 PM
Parakrama Samudra — Farewell Sunset ⭐
One final walk along the ancient 12th-century reservoir. Watch for elephants coming to drink at dusk. The golden light on the water is the perfect end to this extraordinary journey. 90 min.
✨ Gal Viharaya is the single most impressive archaeological sight of the entire trip. Give it at least 90 minutes and arrive early for the best experience.

Practical Advice

Essential Travel Tips

🎫
UNESCO Ticket
Buy the Cultural Triangle ticket at your first site — it covers both Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa and saves significant money.
👘
Dress Code
Shoulders and knees must be covered at all temples. Carry a sarong as backup. Remove shoes at most sacred sites.
🛺
Transport Tips
Anuradhapura: negotiate a full-day tuk-tuk rate (2,500–3,500 LKR). Polonnaruwa: rent a bicycle (300–500 LKR/day) — far better for the flat terrain.
☀️
Beat the Heat
Start mornings early (7–8 AM) and plan for the 12–2 PM heat. Carry water at all times and wear light, breathable clothing.
📸
Photography
No selfies with Buddha statues — it is strictly enforced. No flash photography inside Thivanka Image House. Evening Ruwanweli is spectacular for photos.
💰
Budget Guide
Tuk-tuk full day: 2,500–3,500 LKR. Bicycle: 300–500 LKR. Local lunch: 500–1,200 LKR. Entry tickets: covered by UNESCO pass.
